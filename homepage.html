About Us

Litan Construction Corp. is a privately owned New York based company founded in 2005. We specialize in the following sectors of construction: residential, commercial, hospitality, retail complexes, and restoration projects. Litan Corp. is driven by a team of highly skilled project managers, architects, and interior designers all dedicated to supporting the company's core values: client commitment, strategic analysis, and collaborative design process. Whatever the size of the project, our objective is to bring life to a vision by working closely with the client and community.
